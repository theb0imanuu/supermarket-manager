About Super POS

Super POS System

Version 1.0.0

A web-based Point of Sale system designed for small supermarkets.

Features:
  • Product inventory management
  • Checkout process with cart functionality
  • Product search and filtering
  • Receipt generation
  • Basic reporting (sales, inventory)
  • Multiple payment methods

Built with Flask and Vanilla JavaScript.
